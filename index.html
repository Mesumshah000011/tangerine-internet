What's Good About Tangerine Internet?

1. Easy on the Wallet

Tangerine Internet has a solid reputation for being budget-friendly. It's a good pick for students, small families, and anyone trying to keep bills low without giving up on speed.

2. Six-Month Discount Upfront

They usually knock down the price for the first six months. After that, the cost goes back to normal, but it still stays on the lower end compared to others.

3. No Long-Term Contracts

If you don't want to stay tied down to a contract, Tangerine's plans let you leave whenever you want. No fees, no hassle.

4. Try It for 14 Days — No Pressure

You get a full two weeks to try out the service. Don't like it? Cancel within 14 days and get your money back. Simple as that.

5. Decent Speeds for Everyday Stuff

Tangerine Internet offers different NBN speed options depending on what you need:

  • NBN 25: Light use like browsing and email
  • NBN 50: Great for streaming and small households
  • NBN 100: Perfect for big families or anyone who's always online

6. Bring Your Own Modem

Already have a modem? Use it and save money. If not, you can buy one from them.

Why People Like More Internet

1. Plans Without the Fluff

More Internet keeps it simple. No confusing bundles or hidden fees. Just straight-up NBN plans with clear pricing.

2. Save Big with CommBank

If you bank with CommBank, you can get up to 30% off for an entire year. That's a pretty strong reason to consider More Internet.

3. Business Options Are Available

Need NBN for your home office or business? They've got options with static IPs and faster support for business users.

4. Fast Speeds, Even at Peak Time

You get fast speeds just like Tangerine — up to 100 Mbps depending on your plan. And they hold up well, even in the evening.

5. BYO or Buy a Modem

Bring your own modem if you've got one. Or just order one from their website, pre-configured and ready to go.
